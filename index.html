Tamago sushi
たまご寿司
Ingredients:
  • frozen octopus
  • Kombu (Japanese kelp)
  • Daikon (Japanese radish)
  • sea salt
  • water
Recipe:
  • Step 1: Put on a pot of water, about a gallon, and add a good sized piece of kombu and about 2 oz of sea salt. Bring to a boil.
  • Step 2: Thaw octopus in cold water, then drain well.
  • Step 3: Knead the octopus with fresh grated/chopped daikon radish and sea salt. While doing that, turn the head inside out and check for any leftover viscera. Remove any that’s found, then return the head sac to it’s correct shape.
  • Step 4: Dunk the octopus into the boiling water a few times until the tentacles curl up. Lower the heat a bit, and then simmer the octopus for about 5-10 minutes.
  • Step 5: Let the octopus cool in the liquid for at least an hour. Remove the octopus from the water and let it dry a bit. Slice on an angle, then serve as you like.
